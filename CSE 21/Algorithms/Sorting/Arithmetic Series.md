#Algorithm
A sum over a seq of numbers that share a common difference d when they're i distance apart with N terms in total
$$\sum_{i=0}^n a_0 + di = N(\frac{first+last}{2})=(n+1)(\frac{a_0+(a_0+dn)}{2})$$

Examples:
1+2+3+...+10
- first = 1, last = 10
- N = 10
- sum =  $10\frac{(1+10)}{2}$

1+3+5+7+...+15
- first = 1, last = 15
- N = 8
- sum = $8\frac{(1+15)}{2}$
